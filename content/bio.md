I am a MRes student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China, advised by Dr. Salman Ijaz, Dr. Donglei Sun, and Dr. Adam Rushworth. I graduated with a B.E. in Communication Engineering from Zhejiang Wanli University, advised by Dr. Wenlei Yang.

My research focuses on aerial robotics and control theory — building UAV systems that physically interact with the world. My work involves developing disturbance rejection control strategies for rotorcraft platforms and designing vision-based perception systems for autonomous grasping and navigation.
