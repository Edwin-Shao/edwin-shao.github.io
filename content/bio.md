I am an MRes student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China, advised by Prof. Donglei Sun. I graduated with a B.E. in Communication Engineering from Zhejiang Wanli University, advised by Prof. Wenlei Yang.

My research focuses on aerial robotics and aerial manipulation — building UAV systems that physically interact with the world. My work involves integrating parallel manipulators with quadrotor platforms, developing disturbance rejection control strategies, and designing vision-based perception systems for autonomous grasping and navigation.
